Golden-bristle Grass

Setaria sphacelata

A tufted grass, narrow, blue-green leaves. Flowers: Tall golden inflorescences from October to May Value: Attracts birds to the garden, specifically species like the Bronze Mannikins, Common Waxbills, & Weavers, that feed on the seeds. It is also a host plant for various butterfly species. Habitat: Grassland; wetland
